Each of us has something to boast about in life! After thinking a bit, I took the liberty to summarize where I was able to succeed and do something special:

2001 – 2006 Presentations at scientific conferences
As a student, I really wanted to share my knowledge, thoughts and experience and during 5 years of study in Syktyvkar State University I was able to talk at many scientific conferences of regional, local and international scale, mainly dedicated to issues of marketing research and marketing communications.

2003 Additional education with the defense of my own business plan
Being a third year student of the intramural department of the University I initiated entrance to the Institute of education development of the Republic of Komi and successfully completed additional training in “Applied Economics” major. I defended my own business plan, being able to see prospects of opening coffee shops in the city of Syktyvkar ahead of time.

2005 Initiated completion of the course of legal literacy in “Consultant plus” company
In-depth course was called “Consultant plus, technology 3000”. I passed certification and got the “Professional” certificate.

2006 Organizer of media research,the largest in the Komi Republic
I acted as the organizer of first large-scale research in the Komi Republicof media preferences of residents of all cities of the Komi Republic, the sample size amounted to 8,000 respondents.

2007 Coordinated “Renaissance Baltic Hotel” consulting project
Interesting project, organized by the working group of master students of the Graduate school of management of St. Petersburg State University was devoted to questions of increase of motivation among employees.

2007 Initiated studying the German language in Germany
I graduated from German language courses at the University of Cologne, Wirtschafts - und SozialwissenschaftlicheFakultät, received B2 Certificate

2008 A consulting project in Sweden forthe Redcats Groupinternational holding
I participated in a consulting project, implemented in Sweden for the Redcats Groupinternational holding, one of the leading European companies for the sale of clothes and shoes over the internet. The project was devoted to minimization of costs of consumer returns.

2009-2011 Teaching two author’s courses in the University
I taught two author’s disciplines in the field of marketing for 2 years on Saturdaysat the Syktyvkar State University. In addition, I acted as a member of the State Attestation Commission.

2011 Training and getting the status of certified ISO 19011 auditor
State academy of industrial management named after N. P. Pastukhov, “Internal auditor of quality management systems in accordance with the requirements of international ISO 19011 standard” certificate. Multiple internal audits of the production facility.

2012-2013 Project manager for Kimberly-Clark’s contract manufacturing
Open joint stock production company “STG”, where I acted as key project manager from the side of JSC “Syktyvkar Tissue Group”, was viewed by Kimberly-Clark international companyas a production base for the production of Kleenex toilet paperon the territory of the Russian Federation. The launch of contract manufacturing was deployed for 9 months and required a substantial rebuild of business processes of OJSC “Syktyvkar Tissue Group” in matters of quality control, productionsafety and inventory management. The project was successfully transferred to the working phase.

2014-2017 Standardization and regulation of procurement procedures in the world’s largest company producing steelrainwater systems and metal tile roofing
Within two years I was able to standardize key procurement procedures of Metal Profilegroup of companies, which consistsof 17 factories and more than 70 branches across Russia and the CIStoday.
