Good day!


My name is Viktor. Born in 1983 in a small and beautiful town of Syktyvkar, I had a long way to go. I studied a lot, traveled to different countries, communicated with people, tested myself in science, worked until late at night. The main thing I realized during this time was that I do need continuous development. When I stop, I immediately lose the meaning and interest in events. But any new initiative, especially a complex one, having a visible result ahead, makes me think and act, create and dream, every day getting closer to my goal.


Meanwhile, today’s Viktor is an adult, having high reputation in my field, a respected team leader, but every day I look for and find something new, interesting, important around me and I get involved and bring the project to the result. Out of curiosity and excitement.


Thus, being still a student of Syktyvkar State University, I was employed by a wonderful “Europa plus” radio station, where I researched media audience and organized exciting concerts and social events on behalf of the radio station. At the same time, I actively explored all aspects of marketing, subscribed to industry magazines and books, was an ardent participant of online forums, made presentations at regional, national and international conferences on marketing research and communications. The same passion for continuous development pushed me after graduation from the University and getting higher education to continue my studies in the English language master degree program at Graduate School of Management of St. Petersburg State University, and then even inspired me to relocate to Western Europe, where I graduated from the University of Cologne in Germany and Stockholm School of Economics in Sweden with great interest.


Future career pulled me away from marketing and sent me to procurement. However, even during this intense period of my life I did not forget of my favorite marketing philosophy andI taught marketing disciplines at Syktyvkar State University for two years on Saturdays as a hobby.


I feel very comfortable in procurement, there is a lot of analysis here, relationship management with participants of the value chain, many opportunities to bring tangible benefits to the company and affect the competitiveness of the business. My development did not stop also in JSC “Syktyvkar Tissue group” where on top of procurement, I got involved in managing manufacturing logistics, paid much attention to products quality aspects and increase of production efficiency.


Later on, after moving to Moscow in the beginning of 2014, my destiny was to replace usual pulp and paper industry with metallurgy and metal processing. After I spent some time to adapt to new materials and organizational structures, I got interested in taking part in the centralization and unification of all procurement procedures in russian manufacturing companies.


I made this website mostly for myself, sometimes it’s interesting to take a detached look at my life, sum up the results and contemplate on the further course of my story. What am I interested in now? Of course, the most complicated projects and the most daring challenges. Out of curiosity and excitement.
